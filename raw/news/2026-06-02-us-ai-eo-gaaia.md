- EO "Promoting Advanced AI Innovation and Security" (Jun 2): framework for secure deployment of frontier models; voluntary early gov access (up to 30 days pre-release); AI-cybersecurity clearinghouse. No licensing/preclearance requirement.
- GAAIA discussion draft (Jun 4, Reps. Obernolte & Trahan): ~270 pages; focus on frontier governance, workforce, cybersecurity, R&D; targets large foundation-model developers.
- Together: federal centralization of frontier-model rules, heaviest obligations on frontier developers.
