title: HR shifts from AI copilots to autonomous agents (2026 surveys)
outlet: Korn Ferry / SHRM State of AI in HR 2026
url: https://www.shrm.org/topics-tools/research/state-of-ai-hr-2026/full-report
date: 2026-06-18
captured: 2026-06-21
key_points:
- >50% of talent-acquisition leaders plan to add autonomous AI agents to recruiting teams in 2026 (Korn Ferry) - sourcing, screening, initial engagement end-to-end.
- ~39% of HR teams have adopted AI; recruiting is the #1 HR use case (~27%).
- 88% of HR leaders say teams haven't seen significant business value yet; barriers are organizational (job-loss fear, budget, compliance).
- Directly relevant to ADP (payroll/HR): autonomous agents in HR raise governance + trust demands.
