KEY FACTS / CLAIMS (as reported):
- Thesis: AI must be governed as an economic system priced in tokens (the new unit of value); spend is inherently variable and often unpredictable.
- AI is the fastest-growing line in tech budgets — up to half of IT spend at some firms.
- Enterprise cloud bills rose ~19% in 2025.
- Nearly half of leaders expect up to 3 years to see ROI from basic AI automation; only 28% of global finance leaders report clear measurable value from AI (Deloitte 2025 US Tech Value survey).
- Deloitte simulation: on-prem "AI factory" can deliver >50% cost savings over 3 years vs API/neocloud above a token threshold; ~50% of AI-factory cost is non-GPU (power, cooling, networking, facilities, software).
- Prescription: right-size models, context-window limits, real-time monitoring, budget alerts/chargebacks, explicit FinOps practice.
