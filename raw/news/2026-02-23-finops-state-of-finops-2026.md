KEY FACTS (as reported):
- 98% of respondents now manage AI spend — up from 63% (2025) and 31% (2024).
- 90% manage SaaS (up from 65%); 64% licensing (up from 49%); 57% private cloud (up from 39%).
- AI-cost management is the single most-desired skillset — cited by 58% of respondents.
- FinOps Foundation changed its mission from "managing the Value of Cloud" to "managing the Value of Technology."
- Org placement (per CIO Dive on the data): 78% of teams report to CTO/CIO (up from 61% in 2023); CFO-aligned down to ~8%; CTO/CIO-aligned teams report 2–4× more influence over technology selection.
- Context: Gartner projects global IT spend +9.8% YoY to surpass $6T in 2026.
