# arXiv 2025 Census — Citation-ranked

Citations from **Semantic Scholar** (retrieved 2026-06-22), merged into
`census-2025.jsonl` as `citationCount`, `influentialCitationCount`, `velocity`
(cites ÷ months since publication). Coverage 11,981 / 12,046 (99%).

## Caveats (read first)
- **Citation lag:** Jan-2025 papers had ~17 months to accrue cites, Dec-2025 ~6. Raw ranking favors older papers — use **velocity** for a recency-fair view.
- **Model reports leak in:** the term filter catches LLM model/technical reports (Qwen3, Gemini 2.5, GPT-5…) that cite-massively but aren't agent research. The **'agentic only'** lists below exclude them — that's the useful radar view.
- **Extreme skew:** median 2 cites, mean 9.5; 2,940 papers have 0. Weak signal in the long tail.
- **Influential citations** (citations that build on the work) is a better quality proxy than raw count.
