# POLARIS: Typed Planning and Governed Execution for Agentic AI in Back-Office Automation

**Why it matters:** Governed back-office automation as typed plan synthesis: planner emits type-checked DAGs, a rubric picks one compliant plan, and compiled policy guardrails block side effects before they occur. Audit-grade by design — directly relevant to finance/HR agents.

## Takeaways
- [[polaris|POLARIS]]: planner proposes type-checked DAGs, rubric-guided module selects one compliant plan, execution guarded by validator gates + bounded repair + compiled policy guardrails.
- Produces decision-grade artifacts and full execution traces while cutting human intervention.
- SROIE micro-F1 0.81; 0.95–1.00 precision for anomaly routing with preserved audit trails on a synthetic finance suite.
