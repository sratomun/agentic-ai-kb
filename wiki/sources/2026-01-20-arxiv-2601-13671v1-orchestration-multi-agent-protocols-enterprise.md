# The Orchestration of Multi-Agent Systems: Architectures, Protocols, and Enterprise Adoption

**Why it matters:** The clearest 2026 statement that [[mcp]] (tool access) and [[a2a-protocol|Agent2Agent]] (peer coordination) are complementary halves of the enterprise agent substrate — exactly the interoperability layer the exec needs to track.

## Takeaways
- Treats MCP (tool/context access) and A2A (peer negotiation/delegation) as a combined communication substrate for distributed agent collectives.
- Integrates planning, policy enforcement, state management, and quality ops into one orchestration layer with observability/governance baked in.
- Pitched as an implementation-ready blueprint for enterprise-scale agent ecosystems.
