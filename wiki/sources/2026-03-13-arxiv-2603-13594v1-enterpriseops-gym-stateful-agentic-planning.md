# EnterpriseOps-Gym: Environments and Evaluations for Stateful Agentic Planning and Tool Use in Enterprise Settings

**Why it matters:** A hard enterprise benchmark: 164 DB tables, 512 tools, 1,150 tasks across 8 verticals. Top model (Claude Opus 4.5) only 37.4% — and agents rarely refuse infeasible tasks. Concrete evidence agents aren't enterprise-ready.

## Takeaways
- [[enterpriseops-gym|EnterpriseOps-Gym]]: containerized sandbox, 164 tables, 512 tools, 1,150 expert tasks across 8 verticals (Customer Service, HR, IT, …).
- 14 frontier models: top is Claude Opus 4.5 at 37.4% success; oracle human plans add 14–35pp → strategic reasoning is the bottleneck.
- Agents frequently fail to refuse infeasible tasks (best 53.9%), risking harmful side effects.
