# Foundation Models in Robotics: A Comprehensive Review of Methods, Models, Datasets, Challenges and Future Research Directions

**SURVEY** · arXiv [2604.15395v1](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.15395v1) · 2026-04-16 · Aggelos Psiris, Vasileios Argyriou, Evangelos K. Markakis, Panagiotis Sarigiannidis et al.

**Why it matters:** The orientation map for embodied agents — far from enterprise work, but the planning/tool/memory techniques cross-pollinate.

## What it surveys
Foundation models in robotics — the shift from single-task systems to general-purpose embodied agents.

## Framework / taxonomy
Traces five research phases and gives a granular taxonomy across FM types (LLMs, VFMs, VLMs, VLAs), architectures, learning paradigms/stages, robotic tasks, and application domains, plus a datasets report.

## Notable points
- Centers cross-embodiment generalization and long-horizon planning as the FM contribution.
- Comprehensive datasets/benchmark inventory for embodied learning.
- Bridges the embodied-AI literature to the broader agent stack.
