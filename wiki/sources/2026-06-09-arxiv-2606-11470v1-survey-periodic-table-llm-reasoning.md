# The Periodic Table of LLM Reasoning: A Structured Survey of Reasoning Paradigms, Methods, and Failure Modes

**SURVEY** · arXiv [2606.11470v1](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.11470v1) · 2026-06-09 · Avinash Anand, Mahisha Ramesh, Avni Mittal, Ashutosh Kumar et al.

**Why it matters:** A wide-angle map of reasoning research — useful for placing the agentic-reasoning thread inside the larger reasoning landscape.

## What it surveys
LLM reasoning across paradigms, synthesizing 300+ recent papers.

## Framework / taxonomy
A structured taxonomy spanning Chain-of-Thought, multi-hop, mathematical, commonsense, visual/temporal, code/algorithmic, retrieval-augmented, tool-augmented/agentic, and RL-based reasoning, with methodological trends and a failure-mode synthesis.

## Notable points
- Names recurring failure modes: reasoning hallucinations, brittle multi-step inference, weak causal abstraction, poor cross-domain generalization.
- Connects reasoning paradigms to prompting, architectures, training objectives, reward modeling, and benchmarks.
- Emerging directions: meta-reasoning, self-evolving reasoning, multimodal, socially-grounded reasoning.
