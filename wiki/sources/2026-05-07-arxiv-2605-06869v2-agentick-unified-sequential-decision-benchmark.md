# Agentick: A Unified Benchmark for General Sequential Decision-Making Agents

**Why it matters:** A benchmark putting RL, LLM, VLM, hybrid, and human agents on common ground — finds no paradigm dominates and a reasoning harness multiplies LLM performance 3–10×. Useful corrective to LLM-only framing.

## Takeaways
- [[agentick|Agentick]]: 37 procedurally-generated tasks, 6 capability categories, 4 difficulties, 5 observation modalities via one Gymnasium-compatible interface.
- 90k+ episodes: GPT-5 mini leads overall (0.309 oracle-normalized) but PPO dominates planning/multi-agent; reasoning harness multiplies LLM perf 3–10×; ASCII observations beat natural language.
- No single approach dominates — large headroom across all agent paradigms.
