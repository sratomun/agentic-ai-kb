# Agentic Reasoning for Large Language Models

**Why it matters:** A roadmap survey organizing agentic reasoning into foundational → self-evolving → collective layers, and separating in-context from post-training reasoning. Good scaffolding for how the 2026 threads fit together.

## Takeaways
- Three layers: foundational single-agent reasoning (planning/tool use/search), self-evolving reasoning (feedback/memory/adaptation), collective multi-agent reasoning.
- Distinguishes in-context reasoning (test-time orchestration) from post-training reasoning (RL/SFT).
- Synthesizes frameworks across science/robotics/healthcare/math; open challenges include long-horizon interaction, world modeling, scalable multi-agent training, governance.
