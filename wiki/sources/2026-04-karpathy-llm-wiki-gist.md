# Andrej Karpathy — "LLM wiki" gist

**Gist** · ~April 2026 · Andrej Karpathy

**Why it matters:** The crisp articulation of the pattern this whole radar runs on, and the seed [[open-knowledge-format|OKF]] formalizes.

## The idea
- Keep knowledge as a growing markdown library agents read and update themselves, while humans curate and manage it like code.
- The key argument: *"LLMs don't get bored, don't forget to update a cross-reference, and can touch 15 files in one pass."* The bookkeeping that makes humans abandon personal wikis is exactly what agents are good at.
- The pattern recurs under many names — Obsidian vaults wired to coding agents, the `AGENTS.md`/`CLAUDE.md` convention family, repos of `index.md` + `log.md` that agents consult before doing real work.
