# Text2Cypher dataset (Neo4j)

*The standard 44,387-instance NL-question / Cypher / schema dataset that made text-to-Cypher a benchmarkable task.*

## What it is
Neo4j's consolidated Text2Cypher corpus (39,554 train / 4,833 test), unifying functional, synthetic, crowdsourced, and prior datasets; evaluated with Google-BLEU, Exact-Match, and execution.

## Why it matters
The reference dataset for [[text-to-cypher]] — the graphs' equivalent of Spider/BIRD, and the substrate the schema-filtering and synthetic-data work builds on.
