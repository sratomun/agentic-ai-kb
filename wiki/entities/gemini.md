# Gemini

*Google DeepMind's multimodal frontier model family — the dominant baseline for multimodal reasoning, tool use, and computer-use agent tasks in the 2026 research corpus.*

## What it is
Gemini is Google DeepMind's multimodal LLM family, spanning models from Nano to Ultra. It appears as an agent backbone and evaluation baseline in ~288 of the 2026 H1 census papers — the most-cited model family in the corpus — covering image-and-text reasoning, code, and computer-use tasks.

## Why it matters
Gemini's multimodal and computer-use agent capabilities define the competitive floor that enterprise AI platforms need to match. Performance gaps vs Gemini are the metric enterprise buyers increasingly use to evaluate alternative agent deployments.
