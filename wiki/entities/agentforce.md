# Agentforce

*Salesforce's enterprise platform for building, deploying, and managing custom AI agents on top of its CRM and data stack.*

## What it is
Agentforce is Salesforce's product layer for enterprise agent deployment — letting teams build custom agents that automate CRM workflows, customer service, and back-office tasks using Salesforce's data and tool ecosystem. The $3.6B acquisition of Fin (June 2026) folds a battle-tested service-agent into the platform.

## Why it matters
Agentforce is the platform to beat in enterprise agentic AI. The Fin acquisition signals aggressive consolidation — standalone customer-service agent vendors are at exit risk, and ADP-adjacent HR/CX workflows are being absorbed into the big three suites.
