# ReTool

*Reinforcement learning for strategic tool use in long reasoning — teaching models when and how to invoke tools.*

## What it is
ReTool is a reinforcement learning method that trains LLMs to selectively and strategically invoke tools (code interpreters, search, calculators) within long reasoning chains, rather than calling tools on a fixed schedule.

## Why it matters
Tool-use reliability is the bottleneck for production agents. ReTool's approach — training strategic tool selection with RL rather than heuristics — directly addresses the failure mode where agents call tools too often, too rarely, or in the wrong order.
