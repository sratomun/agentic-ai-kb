# Fin

*AI customer-service agent (formerly Intercom) that resolves queries across chat, WhatsApp, SMS, phone, and Slack — built on its own "Apex" model.*

## What it is
Fin is a conversational AI agent for customer service, operating across live chat, WhatsApp, SMS, phone calls, and Slack. It uses an internal model ("Apex") and an orchestrating "Operator" agent for complex workflows. Salesforce acquired it in June 2026 for ~$3.6B; leadership (McCabe, Traynor) stays on and the team folds into Agentforce.

## Why it matters
The $3.6B exit validates that proven, production-grade service-agent point solutions command premium multiples — and signals the consolidation threat to category-independent agents. Enterprises betting on standalone vendors in this space carry real acquisition/disruption risk.
