# Neo4j

*Graph-database company behind Cypher; author of the standard Text2Cypher dataset and much of the text-to-Cypher research.*

## What it is
Neo4j builds the leading property-graph database and the Cypher query language, and publishes the consolidated Text2Cypher dataset, schema-filtering, and multi-database research.

## Why it matters
The center of gravity for [[text-to-cypher]] research and tooling — track it as the natural-language-to-graph interface matures.

- creator of [[cypher]] and [[text2cypher-dataset]]
- works on [[text-to-cypher]]
