# SAP

*Enterprise software vendor whose researchers published a production-scale study comparing orchestration architectures for autonomous multi-agent systems.*

## What it is
SAP SE researchers (Dhanyamraju, Raghav, Lee) ran a controlled comparison of DAG Plan & Execute vs [[react|ReAct]] orchestration across 208 production-derived enterprise scenarios, finding scale (not task complexity) is the dominant performance variable.

## Why it matters
SAP's production-scale research grounds enterprise-agent claims in real scenarios — and the conclusion that scale dominates means enterprises deploying agents at volume need robust failure-handling more than sophisticated planning.
