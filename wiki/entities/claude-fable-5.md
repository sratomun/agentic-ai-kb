# Claude Fable 5

*Anthropic's consumer-facing frontier model, layered on Claude Mythos 5 — disabled globally in June 2026 under a US export-control directive.*

## What it is
Claude Fable 5 is Anthropic's product-tier frontier model, built on top of the more powerful [[claude-mythos-5]] base. In June 2026, the US Commerce Department ordered Anthropic to pull both Fable 5 and Mythos 5 after a reported jailbreak exposed Mythos's cybersecurity capabilities; Anthropic complied under protest. Lower-tier models (Opus 4.8 and below) were unaffected.

## Why it matters
The first known US government-enforced model shutdown — a precedent that regulators can compel frontier labs to pull models globally, independent of the lab's own risk assessment. Signals that export-control reach now extends to AI capabilities, not just chips.
