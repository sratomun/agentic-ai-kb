# Constitutional AI

*Anthropic's approach to alignment: a model critiques and revises its own outputs against a written set of principles (a "constitution"), replacing much human feedback with AI feedback (RLAIF).*

## What it is
A two-phase method: a supervised phase where the model self-critiques and revises responses against constitutional principles, then an RL phase (RLAIF) where preferences are generated by an AI judge rather than human labelers. It's a [[reward-modeling]] variant that scales the feedback step — and the method most associated with [[anthropic]].

## Why it matters
Constitutional AI is the bet that alignment feedback can be scaled with compute instead of human labor, by writing principles down and letting models apply them. For the exec it's the clearest example of *alignment as an explicit, auditable spec* — and a strategic differentiator Anthropic leans on.
