# OpenAI o3

*OpenAI's frontier reasoning model — a frequent strong baseline for agentic tool use and multi-step reasoning.*

## What it is
OpenAI o3 is the successor to o1 in OpenAI's reasoning model line, achieving substantially higher scores on hard reasoning benchmarks (AIME, GPQA, ARC) and becoming the reference strong-baseline for agentic reasoning and tool-use evaluation.

## Why it matters
o3's benchmark scores are the competitive bar every agentic reasoning system is measured against in 2025–26. Understanding its capabilities and failure modes tells you exactly where the frontier is and where task-specific fine-tuning still pays off.
