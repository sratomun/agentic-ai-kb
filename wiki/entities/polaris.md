# POLARIS

*A governed orchestration framework for enterprise back-office automation: typed plan synthesis, rubric-guided plan selection, and compiled policy guardrails that block side effects before execution.*

## What it is
POLARIS synthesizes type-checked DAG execution plans, uses rubric scoring to select among candidate plans, and compiles policy guardrails that prevent side effects at the plan level — before execution begins. It produces audit-grade artifacts and execution traces as first-class outputs.

## Why it matters
Pre-execution guardrails and audit traces are what regulated industries need before deploying autonomous agents in back-office workflows. POLARIS shows this is achievable at the planning stage — a viable path to governance-compliant agent automation today.
