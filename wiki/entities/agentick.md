# Agentick

*A unified benchmark putting RL, LLM, VLM, hybrid, and human agents on common ground across 37 procedural tasks — finds no paradigm dominates, and reasoning harnesses multiply LLM performance 3–10×.*

## What it is
Agentick uses 37 Gymnasium-compatible procedural tasks to evaluate classical RL, LLM, VLM, hybrid, and human agents on a single footing. Its key findings: no paradigm consistently dominates; adding a reasoning harness to LLMs improves performance 3–10× without scaling the model.

## Why it matters
The cross-paradigm result matters for tooling decisions: pure LLM agents aren't always the right choice, and adding a reasoning harness — not a bigger model — is the highest-leverage improvement available for procedural task performance.
