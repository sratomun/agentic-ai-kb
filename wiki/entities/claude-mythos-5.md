# Claude Mythos 5

*Anthropic's research-grade base frontier model with powerful cybersecurity capabilities — the government's target in the June 2026 export-control shutdown.*

## What it is
Claude Mythos 5 is the underlying foundation model on which [[claude-fable-5]] is built, noted internally for advanced cybersecurity capabilities. In June 2026, a reported jailbreak that could unlock those capabilities triggered a US Commerce Department export-control directive; Anthropic disabled Mythos 5 and Fable 5 globally for all users in response.

## Why it matters
Mythos is the case study for capability-triggered regulation: a specific technical capability (cyber) in a base model led directly to a government kill-switch order. Establishes that regulators will target base models, not just their product-layer derivatives.
