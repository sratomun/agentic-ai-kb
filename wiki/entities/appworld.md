# AppWorld

*A high-fidelity benchmark of realistic app/API workflows — agents must navigate real application environments by writing and executing code.*

## What it is
AppWorld provides a simulated environment of real applications (email, calendar, contacts, etc.) with realistic APIs, requiring agents to complete multi-step tasks by calling APIs via generated code. Appears in ~29 of the 2026 H1 census papers as a standard for interactive coding and tool-use evaluation.

## Why it matters
Most enterprise agent tasks live in integrated API-first environments like AppWorld — strong performance here is a prerequisite for production deployment in business workflows that span multiple internal systems.
