# Salesforce

*Enterprise CRM and application platform vendor aggressively building out its [[agentforce]] agent stack through M&A and integration.*

## What it is
Salesforce is an enterprise cloud platform for CRM, sales, and service. Its Agentforce product is its agent layer, built on top of Salesforce data and workflows. In June 2026, Salesforce acquired Fin for ~$3.6B (expected close Q4 FY2027) to fold a production-grade customer-service agent directly into Agentforce; leadership (McCabe, Traynor) stays on.

> Note: earlier draft listed M3ter and Contentful acquisitions — those are not backed by any captured source and were removed pending verification.

## Why it matters
The most aggressive platform bundler in enterprise agentic AI: buy proven point-solution agents and lock the workflows inside the Salesforce ecosystem. Standalone customer-service agent vendors are now acquisition targets; procurement bets on independents carry consolidation risk.
