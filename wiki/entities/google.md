# Google

*The parent company of Google DeepMind — relevant here for its open Gemma models and as infrastructure partner for the Vera Rubin AI compute platform.*

## What it is
Google is the parent company of Google DeepMind (frontier AI lab) and the maker of the open Gemma model family. It is named as a Vera Rubin platform launch partner offering compute infrastructure from H2 2026.

## Why it matters
Google operates on both the model layer (Gemini, open Gemma) and the cloud infrastructure layer simultaneously — making it the most integrated AI stack player. Its Vera Rubin partnership places it in the agent-compute economics story alongside NVIDIA.
