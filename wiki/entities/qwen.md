# Qwen

*Alibaba's open-weight LLM family — the most-used base model in 2026 agent research, especially for RL post-training.*

## What it is
Qwen is Alibaba's open-weight LLM family, covering parameter sizes from 0.5B to 72B+. With permissive weights and strong tool-use, it became the de-facto standard base model for agentic RL research in 2025–26. Mentioned in ~469 of 2026 H1 census papers.

## Why it matters
Qwen's dominance in academic agent research means it's the model with the most post-training recipes, fine-tunes, and evaluation data available. For enterprise teams building task-specific agents on open weights, Qwen is the lowest-friction starting point.
