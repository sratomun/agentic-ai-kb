# FinOps Foundation

*The standards body for cloud (now technology) financial operations — and the institution turning "FinOps for AI" and token economics into a formal discipline.*

## What it is
A Linux Foundation project that defines the FinOps Framework, certifications, and practitioner community for managing the value of technology spend. In 2025–26 it extended the framework to AI: a "FinOps for AI" Scope, AI Working Group papers (AI Cost Estimation; How to Forecast AI), a "tokenomics" articulation of the token as the atomic unit of AI value, and a FinOps Certified: FinOps for AI training track (rolling out from June 2025). It formally widened its mission from "managing the Value of Cloud" to "managing the Value of *Technology*."

## Why it matters
The Foundation is the evidence that agent cost has institutionalized past hype: its State of FinOps 2026 survey (n≈1,192) reports **98% of practitioners now manage AI spend (up from 63%, then 31% in the two prior years)** and names AI-cost management the most-wanted skill (58%) ([[2026-02-23-finops-state-of-finops-2026|State of FinOps 2026]]). So what: it supplies the shared vocabulary (Scopes, tokenomics, unit economics) the [[agent-finops]] discipline is converging on — read its numbers as directionally strong but self-reported from a self-selected membership sample.
