# GPT-4o / GPT-5

*OpenAI's frontier model family — the dominant closed-model baseline in agent benchmarks and the most-used agent backbone in deployment studies.*

## What it is
GPT-4o and GPT-5 are OpenAI's frontier models, used as the default strong-baseline in agent evaluations and as the backbone in most enterprise deployment studies. Mentioned in ~462 of 2026 H1 census papers, they set the bar that other models aim to beat.

## Why it matters
