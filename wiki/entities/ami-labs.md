# AMI Labs (Advanced Machine Intelligence)

*Yann LeCun's Paris-headquartered AI lab (founded ~early 2026) building world models / JEPA as an explicit alternative to LLMs.*

## What it is
A frontier AI company — "ami" = friend in French — co-founded by [[yann-lecun]] (Exec Chairman) and Alex LeBrun (CEO). Pitched as a credible frontier lab "neither Chinese nor American," betting on world models trained on video/audio/sensor data rather than text. Reporting puts its seed at ~$1.03B (≈$3.5B pre-money, announced March 2026 — among Europe's largest).

## Why it matters
The best-capitalized **contrarian bet against the LLM paradigm** — a concrete, well-funded test of LeCun's claim that human-level AI and reliable agents require world models, not scaled LLMs. A node to watch as the world-models-vs-LLM debate plays out in the market, not just on stage.
