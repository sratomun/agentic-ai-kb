# Vera Rubin

*NVIDIA's agentic-AI compute platform — explicitly built for reasoning and agent inference at ~10x better efficiency than Blackwell.*

## What it is
Vera Rubin is NVIDIA's next-generation AI compute platform announced at GTC March 2026, unifying seven chips (Vera CPU, Rubin GPU, NVLink 6, ConnectX-9, BlueField-4, Spectrum-6, Groq 3 LPU) into an AI supercomputer. Claimed ~10x inference throughput per watt and ~1/10 cost per token vs Blackwell. Partner products ship H2 2026.

## Why it matters
Vera Rubin directly addresses the inference cost barrier for production agents. If it ships as claimed (volume Q1 2027), it should make always-on agentic workflows economically viable at scale — changing the ROI calculus for enterprise agent investments.
