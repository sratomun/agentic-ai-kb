# BrowseComp

*A challenging benchmark for web-browsing agents requiring hard-to-find multi-hop facts.*

## What it is
BrowseComp is an OpenAI benchmark of difficult questions that require multi-hop web browsing to answer — designed to test deep retrieval, not just surface search. It became the standard for evaluating deep research and web-browsing agents.

## Why it matters
Enterprise AI assistants are increasingly expected to do autonomous research. BrowseComp is the benchmark that separates "search-and-summarize" from genuine investigative browsing — the capability gap the exec needs to understand when evaluating AI research tools.
