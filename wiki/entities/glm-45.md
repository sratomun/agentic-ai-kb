# GLM-4.5

*An open foundation model explicitly designed for Agentic, Reasoning, and Coding tasks — a credible open alternative to closed frontier models.*

## What it is
GLM-4.5 is an open foundation model series from Zhipu AI explicitly designed for Agentic, Reasoning, and Coding (ARC) workloads. It ships both text and vision variants with strong agentic benchmarks.

## Why it matters
GLM-4.5 widens the field of capable open models available for agentic deployment, giving enterprises a non-US, non-Chinese-closed alternative for coding and reasoning agents — relevant for teams evaluating open-weight options post-export-control.
