# Llama

*Meta's open-weight LLM family — the dominant open base model for agent fine-tuning, on-prem deployments, and reproducible research.*

## What it is
Llama is Meta's family of openly-released, commercially-licensed LLMs, ranging from 7B to 70B+ parameters. It appears in ~168 of the 2026 H1 census papers as a base model for agent fine-tuning, agentic RL experiments, and on-premise deployments where data residency or cost rules out frontier APIs.

## Why it matters
Llama sets the ceiling on what on-prem agents can do — its capabilities cap enterprise deployments that can't use closed frontier models due to compliance, cost, or data-residency constraints. When Llama narrows the gap with closed models, on-prem agent deployment becomes more viable.
