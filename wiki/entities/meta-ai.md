# Meta AI (FAIR)

*Meta's AI organization — home of FAIR (founded by Yann LeCun) and the open-weight Llama family.*

## What it is
Meta's research and product AI arm: **FAIR** (Fundamental AI Research, founded and long led by [[yann-lecun]]) and the [[llama|Llama]] open-weight model line. A central force in open-weight AI, though reporting in 2025–26 describes traction struggles with Llama and internal shake-ups (the ScaleAI acquisition; LeCun's late-2025 departure to found [[ami-labs]]).

## Why it matters
The standard-bearer for *open-weight* frontier AI in the West — its trajectory is a bellwether for whether open or closed wins, a live thread in [[debate-llms-path-to-agi]] (where LeCun argues closed is "a strategic mistake"). FAIR is also where JEPA / [[world-models]] research originated.
