# Kimi K2

*Moonshot's open agentic foundation model — tuned for tool use and long-horizon tasks, a strong open alternative to closed models.*

## What it is
Kimi K2 is an open agentic foundation model from Moonshot AI, specifically tuned for tool use and long-horizon agentic tasks. It competes with GLM-4.5 and closed models on agentic benchmarks.

## Why it matters
Kimi K2 extends the roster of capable open models for enterprise agent deployment, particularly for teams that can't use US-origin closed models due to data-residency or export-control constraints.
