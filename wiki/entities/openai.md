# OpenAI

*The frontier AI lab behind GPT-5 and the Agents SDK — Anthropic's primary competitor and the standard enterprise baseline.*

## What it is
OpenAI is the frontier AI lab that produces the GPT-5 family, the Agents SDK, Responses API, and ChatGPT. Named (with Anthropic, Meta, Mistral) as a frontier lab planning to run on NVIDIA's Vera Rubin platform; Sam Altman was quoted on running 'more powerful models and agents at massive scale.'

## Why it matters
OpenAI is the default enterprise comparison point for Claude. Its Agents SDK and practical guide define the reference architecture most enterprise teams start from — knowing where it leads and where Claude leads is essential for model-selection decisions.
