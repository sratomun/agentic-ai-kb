# CrewAI

*A role-based multi-agent framework for composing collaborative agent 'crews' — lightweight, Python-native, fast to prototype.*

## What it is
CrewAI lets developers define specialist agents by role and goal, then assemble them into a coordinated crew that collaborates on a shared task. Appears in ~13 of the 2026 H1 census papers; popular for rapid prototyping of multi-agent workflows.

## Why it matters
CrewAI's simplicity makes it the entry point for enterprises starting to experiment with multi-agent orchestration — its adoption in the research corpus signals it's a common first framework teams reach for before graduating to more stateful solutions like [[langgraph]].
