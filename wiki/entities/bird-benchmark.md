# BIRD

*A large-scale, real-database text-to-SQL benchmark — the standard yardstick for data-query agents.*

## What it is
BIRD (BIg Bench for LaRge-scale Database Grounded Text-to-SQL Evaluation) tests text-to-SQL on real, messy enterprise schemas with execution accuracy as the metric. Mentioned in ~41 of 2026 H1 census papers.

## Why it matters
Data-query agents are among the highest-ROI enterprise deployments. BIRD's real-database difficulty means a model that passes it is credibly enterprise-ready for analyst-automation use cases.
