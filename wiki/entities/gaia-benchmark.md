# GAIA

*A general-assistant benchmark requiring tool use, browsing, and multi-step reasoning across diverse real-world tasks.*

## What it is
GAIA is a benchmark testing general AI assistant capabilities: tool use, web browsing, file handling, and multi-step reasoning across 466 real-world questions at three difficulty tiers. It measures end-to-end task completion, not isolated capabilities.

## Why it matters
GAIA is the closest public approximation to 'can this agent actually do knowledge-work tasks?' — the benchmark most directly predictive of enterprise assistant utility. When orchestration approaches score lower than simpler baselines (as in ChromaFlow), GAIA is what surfaces that failure.
