# MultiWOZ

*Multi-domain task-oriented dialogue benchmark — the standard testbed for multi-turn intent and slot understanding.*

## What it is
MultiWOZ is a large multi-domain Wizard-of-Oz dialogue dataset used for dialogue state tracking, intent, and slot filling; later versions (2.3+) clean its annotations.

## Why it matters
The reference for *multi-turn* intent understanding, where ambiguity and intent drift actually appear. Used to show small ontology-augmented models approaching GPT-4 on multi-intent reads.
