# SmolLM

*HuggingFace's fully-open, data-centric family of very small models (125M–1.7B) — the open-weight, open-data end of the SLM spectrum.*

## What it is
A compact model family from HuggingFace (SmolLM / SmolLM2), ranging 125M–1.7B parameters, released with open weights *and* open training data. The emphasis is data-centric training of genuinely small models — the "smol goes big" line — rather than shrinking a proprietary recipe.

## Why it matters
SmolLM anchors the smallest, most democratized corner of [[small-language-models]]: SmolLM2 matches 14B contemporaries and 70B models of two years prior on language understanding, tool calling, and instruction following. For anyone who needs an auditable, on-device, no-API model, this is the reference point — and the open *data* makes it the one researchers can actually build on.
