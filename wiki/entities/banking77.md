# BANKING77

*Fine-grained single-domain intent benchmark — 77 banking intents — testing close-intent disambiguation.*

## What it is
BANKING77 (Casanueva et al., 2020) is an intent-detection dataset of 77 closely related banking intents, a standard test of fine-grained disambiguation.

## Why it matters
Where many real deployments live: lots of near-synonymous intents in one domain. A good probe for whether a cheap classifier can hold accuracy as the label space gets dense.
