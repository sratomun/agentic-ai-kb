# Spider / Spider 2.0

*Cross-domain text-to-SQL benchmarks — Spider 2.0 raising difficulty to enterprise-scale workflows with real warehouses.*

## What it is
Spider is a cross-domain text-to-SQL benchmark; Spider 2.0 raises difficulty to enterprise-scale tasks using real cloud data warehouses (BigQuery, Snowflake) and complex SQL dialects. Mentioned in ~29 of 2026 H1 census papers.

## Why it matters
Spider 2.0's enterprise-scale difficulty makes it a credible proxy for real analyst-automation tasks. BIRD handles messy schemas; Spider 2.0 handles warehouse-scale complexity — together they're the two-benchmark suite for enterprise SQL agent evaluation.
