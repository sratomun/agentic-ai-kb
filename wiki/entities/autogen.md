# AutoGen

*Microsoft's multi-agent conversation framework — structured role-based dialogue between specialist LLM agents.*

## What it is
AutoGen enables structured multi-agent collaboration through a message-passing model where each agent has a defined role and responds to other agents within a conversation thread. Appears in ~19 of the 2026 H1 census papers as a common orchestration baseline for research.

## Why it matters
As a Microsoft product, AutoGen sits naturally alongside Azure and [[windows-365-for-agents]] in enterprise Microsoft stacks — a signal that Microsoft is positioning its entire agent toolchain (from framework to Cloud PC to identity) as an integrated enterprise offering.
