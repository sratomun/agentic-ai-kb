# OpenAI o1

*OpenAI's first inference-time reasoning model — the catalyst for the 2025 reasoning-model wave.*

## What it is
OpenAI o1 is OpenAI's first model trained to 'think' before answering using extended chain-of-thought reasoning at inference time. Released in late 2024, it catalyzed the entire reasoning-model category now occupied by o3, DeepSeek R1, and Claude's extended-thinking models.

## Why it matters
o1 established that inference-time compute is a second scaling axis alongside training compute — a paradigm shift that reshaped how every lab trains and deploys frontier models. Understanding o1 is understanding where the 2025–26 reasoning-model race started.
