# VLA (Vision-Language-Action)

*Vision-Language-Action models — robot-control foundation models mapping visual perception and language instructions to actions.*

## What it is
VLA (Vision-Language-Action) models are foundation models for robot control that take visual inputs and language instructions and output control actions. They are the dominant model class in 2026 robotics agent research. Mentioned in ~104 of 2026 H1 census papers.

## Why it matters
VLAs are the architecture through which LLM intelligence is being transferred into physical systems. For the exec, they're most relevant as a signal of how quickly AI is moving from software automation to physical-world operations.
